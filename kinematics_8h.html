Detailed Description

Contains functions for transforming coordinates and computing velocity in inertial and body-fixed frames.

These functions are essential for converting between the body-fixed frame (BFF) and the inertial (earth) frame, as well as for calculating velocity at any point on the body due to its kinematics.

Transforms coordinates from the body-fixed frame (BFF) to the inertial frame.

The function - [body_fixed_frame_to_inertial_frame] applies a rotation and translation to convert a point from the body-fixed coordinate system to the global inertial frame.
